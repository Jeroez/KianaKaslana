White Comet

White Comet is a playable battlesuit in Honkai Impact 3. It is used during Kiana Kaslana's first appearance in the main story and became an iconic image for the game over time due to constant re-apperances, usually using Prodigal Girl (Icon) Prodigal Girl over the default outfit. As the first battlesuit obtainable by Captains, it features fairly simple gameplay focused on slow-but-heavy combo attacks. It can restore SP upon Ultimate Evasion every 15s, giving it some support usability.



















 Valkyrie Ranger

Valkyrie Ranger is a good option here because she consistently draws large groups of mobs and can apply stuns with most of her attacks. Her ultimate is a powerful AoE, while her QTE skill can deal massive damage to a single target when the enemy is frozen. Her best weapons include Artemis' Guardian and Mjolnir.



















 Void Drifter

Void Drifter is a powerful battlesuit that harnesses the power of the void. Kiana's attacks become more fluid and precise, allowing her to unleash devastating combos on her enemies. She gains enhanced mobility and defensive capabilities, making her a formidable force on the battlefield.



















 Divine Prayer

Divine Prayer is a support-oriented battlesuit that focuses on healing and providing buffs to allies. Kiana gains the ability to heal herself and her teammates, as well as increase their attack and defense stats. She also has access to powerful healing and support skills that can turn the tide of battle in her favor.


























 Knight Moonbeam

Knight Moonbeam is a versatile battlesuit that specializes in ranged combat. Kiana gains access to a powerful laser cannon that can deal massive damage to her enemies from a distance. She also has access to a variety of offensive and defensive skills that allow her to adapt to any situation on the battlefield.



















 Herrscher of the Void

Herrscher of the Void is a transformational battlesuit that grants Kiana immense power at the cost of her humanity. In this form, Kiana gains access to devastating void powers that can decimate her enemies with ease. She becomes nearly invincible, capable of unleashing powerful attacks that can destroy entire armies.



















 Herrscher of Flamescion

Herrscher of Flamescion is a fiery battlesuit that allows Kiana to control the power of flames. She gains access to a variety of fire-based attacks that can burn her enemies to ashes. With this battlesuit, Kiana becomes a force of nature, capable of unleashing torrents of flames that can engulf entire battlefields.



















 Herrscher of Finality

Herrscher of Finality is Kiana's ultimate form, granting her godlike powers over space and time. In this form, Kiana gains access to reality-warping abilities that allow her to manipulate the fabric of the universe itself. She becomes nearly unstoppable, capable of bending reality to her will and reshaping the world as she sees fit.
